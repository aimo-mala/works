A token-centric journey


The mission in LON is to reform the trading experience (multi-chain swap) that's meant to be more lean, intuitive and yet to offer a streamlined experience not only for the existing users but also to those newcomers who just delve into crypto space. Therefore, a token centric web/app platform is intended to connect the scale of Web 2 with the decentralisation of Web 3.0.

DISCOVERY


One of the first things we did as a team was establish who are we going to serve and how are we going to serve them. During discovery with research team, the data indicates that majority of DEX customers are from wallet, hence, to align visual language & brand identity is particular important. Besides, let team to understand the value of a better swap experience could retain the loyalty of existing customers.

Brand assets & Graphics


DeFi crypto trading can be a daunting task for many. Customizable illustrations/characters is an effective way to grab the customer's attention and set the tone of brand identity in the mind of customers.
